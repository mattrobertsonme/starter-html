About Matt Robertson

Skills

Having completed a number of projects now, I have a strong knowledge of HTML, JavaScript and CSS. There are still a number of programming languages that I’d liked to learn including C. In more recent times I’ve started the process of learning Java, PHP, and #C. Although I have a basic understanding of what these languages do and how to actually use them, I have yet to master them to the level of HTML and CSS.

Check out the full range of services I offer on my Freelance page.

Interests and Hobbies

Being from Melbourne, I’m a massive footy fan. I’ve been a member of the North Melbourne Kangaroos since 2013. The regular season usually passes by way too quickly and the off-season seems to drag on forever. Luckily though, in the past couple of seasons North Melbourne has come good in September and delivered two consecutive finals berths.

Building PCs is another hobby of mine. It’s so easy to do once you get the basic know-how,

Being a 23-year-old non-athletic male, gaming is naturally one of my favourite hobbies. Without doubt Grand Theft Auto is hands down my favourite series to play. The openness of the games, the comedic tone, the wonderful stories and voice acting, well-written characters, great gameplay and beautiful scenery are just a few reasons to love the franchise. There are no surprises that Grand Theft Auto 5, the latest game in the series has sold more than 60 million units and is currently the fastest selling entertainment product in history after making $1 billion USD in just the first three games of its release.

Education

In 2014, I completed my Certificate IV in Computer Systems. The course covered a range of topics including networking, troubleshooting, and PC hardware. I learned a lot from the course and constantly put the knowledge I learned from it to use.

Currently, I’m completing my Certificate IV in Web Based Technologies. In the course, I’m learning a variety of subjects including MySQL, Search Engine Optimization, Web Design, Video Production, Image Manipulation and other related web based technologies. It’s a tonne of fun and I’m really enjoying the subjects on offer. At the end of my course in 2017, I hope to secure a job as junior web developer/designer.
